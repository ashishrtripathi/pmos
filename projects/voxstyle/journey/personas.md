# Personas: VOXStyle Video Creator

## Persona 1: Content Creator Sarah

- **Role**: Social Media Content Creator
- **Age**: 28
- **Technical Skill**: Intermediate
- **Goal**: Create professional videos for social media without video editing skills
- **Pain Points**:
  - Traditional video editing is time-consuming
  - Hiring videographers is expensive
  - Existing AI tools produce low-quality output
  - Difficult to maintain brand consistency
- **Context**: Creates 5-10 videos per week for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- **Quote**: "I just want to describe my idea and have a great video ready in minutes."

## Persona 2: Marketing Manager Mike

- **Role**: Small Business Marketing Manager
- **Age**: 35
- **Technical Skill**: Beginner
- **Goal**: Create product demo and promotional videos quickly
- **Pain Points**:
  - No in-house video team
  - Budget constraints for video production
  - Need to iterate quickly on video content
  - Hard to track video performance
- **Context**: Creates 2-5 videos per month for product launches
- **Quote**: "I need to go from idea to published video in under an hour."

## Persona 3: Educator Emma

- **Role**: Online Course Creator
- **Age**: 42
- **Technical Skill**: Intermediate
- **Goal**: Create educational video content efficiently
- **Pain Points**:
  - Complex topics need visual explanation
  - Recording and editing takes too long
  - Need consistent style across course modules
  - Accessibility requirements (captions, clear audio)
- **Context**: Creates weekly educational content for students
- **Quote**: "I want to focus on teaching, not on video production."
