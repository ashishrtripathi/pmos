# Customer Journey — Sarah (Content Creator)

## Sarah's Path: From Idea to Published Video

**Persona**: Social Media Content Creator, 28, Intermediate
**Quote**: "I just want to describe my idea and have a great video ready in minutes."

---

### Journey Steps (Left → Right)

| Step | Activity | Tasks | Pain Points | Screen |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|--------|
| **1. Discovery** | Find a video tool | Search Google, see ads, hear from peers | Hard to judge quality, unclear pricing | Landing Page |
| **2. Sign Up** | Create account | Click "Get Started", fill minimal form | Don't want to commit before trying | Sign Up Form |
| **3. Choose Template** | Pick video style | Browse templates, select "Social Media" | Too many options, hard to tell apart | Template Gallery |
| **4. Enter Subject** | Describe the video | Type topic, pick length | Not sure what level of detail AI needs | Subject Input |
| **5. Generate Script** | Review AI output | Read scene table, edit voiceover lines | AI doesn't match my tone, need to tweak | Scene Table Editor |
| **6. Preview Video** | Watch the render | Play preview, check each scene | Quality not what I expected, want to adjust | Video Preview |
| **7. Edit Scenes** | Customize details | Swap images, change text, adjust timing | Limited editing, hard to be precise | Scene Editor |
| **8. Export & Share** | Publish content | Download MP4, upload to TikTok/Instagram | Watermark, wrong aspect ratio for platform | Export Options |
